Shona moong erKhichudi

Ingredients

  1. moong dal
  2. rice
  3. potatoes
  4. Red Chillies
  5. Mustard oil
  6. Turmeric powder
  7. Salt
  8. 5 phoron

Recepie

  1. peel and wash the potatoes. Boil them in water until they are soft. You can poke the potatoes and check the hardness during boiling.
  2. Chop the onions finely into crescent shapes. Chop the green chillies in to circular rings.
  3. Smash the potatoes into soft dough.
  4. Spread 3 tsb mustard oil on a saute pan and wait till its warm.
  5. Then put two rechillies by breaking them in between so that they dont burst at you.
  6. Once the red chillies are crunchy and brown put the finely chopped onions and green chilliesand fry them until the onions turn golden.
  7. Then put 1/2 tsb of salt and saute them well.
  8. Then put 2/3 tsb turmeric powder and mix them well.
  9. Finally, add the smashed potatoes, mixall of the ingredients very well and then saute them for some time
  10. Serve Alu Sheddho with steamed rice and dal.
